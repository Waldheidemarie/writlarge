New York City’s Educational Past
from the Ground Up

Writ Large contributes to a comprehensive understanding of New York City’s educational past by locating formal and informal sites of teaching and learning in neighborhoods across time and to identify archival resources in New York City repositories, and elsewhere), to document that history.

This site is designed to document the multiple settings in which diverse New Yorkers learned and capture the reciprocal relationship between education and the transformation of the city.
